How it works
ProtonDB mirror — Community reports are fetched daily and stored as static JSON files. Each game has a latest.json with all known reports, accessible under /data/{appId}/.
Proton Pulse configs — Users submit their working Proton configurations (version, launch options, env vars) via the Decky Plugin. Configs are stored in Supabase and displayed alongside ProtonDB reports.
Community voting — Reports can be upvoted or downvoted to surface the most helpful compatibility info. Vote totals are aggregated from Supabase in real-time.
