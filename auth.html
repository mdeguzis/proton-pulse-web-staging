Steam Sign-In

You’ll see Steam’s sign-in page next.

Before we send you there, here’s what to expect: the next page should open on steamcommunity.com, where Steam handles the sign-in directly.

What happens next

  • Steam will ask you to sign in or confirm the account you already use there.
  • After you approve it, Steam sends you back to Proton Pulse already signed in.
  • Proton Pulse never receives your Steam password.

How to verify it’s legitimate

Check that the next page URL starts with https://steamcommunity.com/ before entering anything.

If you are already signed into Steam in this browser, you may only need to click Sign In.
