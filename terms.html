What this is

Proton Pulse is a free, open source project that mirrors ProtonDB community compatibility reports for Steam games and lets users share their own Proton configs. It's a hobby project, not a commercial service.

Provided as-is

This project is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. Compatibility data comes from community reports and may not be accurate. We don't guarantee uptime, data accuracy, or that any particular game will work with Proton.

User contributions

By submitting configs or votes, you agree that your contributions can be displayed publicly on this site. You can delete your submissions from the account management page.

Don't submit content that's abusive, misleading, or violates someone else's rights. We can remove content or accounts that cause problems.

ProtonDB data

Community reports displayed here originate from ProtonDB. Proton Pulse is not affiliated with ProtonDB. Their data is used under fair use for community benefit.

Account deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the account management page.
